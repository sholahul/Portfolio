Sentiment Analysis on The Opinions about Informatics
Using Naive Bayes Method


Informatics is one of the majors that exist in the Computer Science Faculty of Sriwijaya University. The School was established in 2004 and has more than 400 students. The number of students who have or are studying in the informatics make a lot of opinions formed about how it feels to study in this major. Our research will group these opinions. This study utilizes the Naive Bayes method for grouping those opinions.
